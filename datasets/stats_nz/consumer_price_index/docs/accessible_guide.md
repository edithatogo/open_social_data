# Accessible Guide to Consumers Price Index, New Zealand

## What This Data Is About

The CPI tracks how prices change for common goods and services. It helps describe inflation and changes in household costs.

## Why It Matters

People use CPI data to understand cost-of-living pressure, compare price growth over time, and adjust payments or contracts.

## Key Things to Know

CPI is an index, not a direct price list. A higher index means prices are higher than the reference period, but it does not show the exact price of each item.
