# Accessible Guide to Labour Market Statistics, New Zealand

## What This Data Is About

This data shows how many people are working, looking for work, or outside the labour force. It can also show rates such as unemployment and participation.

## Why It Matters

Labour market data helps explain economic conditions and whether people can find work. It is useful for policy, education, business planning, and community services.

## Key Things to Know

Rates and counts are different. A count shows people; a rate shows a percentage of a relevant population. Always check which measure you are using.
