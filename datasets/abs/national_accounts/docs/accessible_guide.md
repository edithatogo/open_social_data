# Accessible Guide to Australian National Accounts

## What This Data Is About

National accounts describe the size and movement of the Australian economy. GDP is one of the best-known measures in this dataset family.

## Why It Matters

These data help explain economic growth, income, spending, production, and broad economic conditions.

## Key Things to Know

National accounts contain many related measures. Always check whether values are current prices, volume measures, seasonally adjusted, trend, or original.
