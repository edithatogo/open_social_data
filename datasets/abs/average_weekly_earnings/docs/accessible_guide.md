# Accessible Guide to Average Weekly Earnings

**Source:** Australian Bureau of Statistics  
**Guide Updated:** 2026-06-23

## What is This Data About?

Average Weekly Earnings measures how much employees earn on average in Australia, using ABS-published earnings series. The selected series may vary by sex, state or territory, sector, and type of earnings.

## Why is This Data Important?

Earnings data helps people understand wage levels and changes over time. It can support labour-market reporting, cost-of-living analysis, and comparisons between sectors or regions.

## How Can You Use This Data?

You might compare ordinary-time weekly earnings across states, or examine whether public and private sector earnings have changed differently over time. Always compare the same earnings measure and the same population.

## Key Things to Know Before Using This Data

Average earnings are not the same as median earnings, and they can be affected by very high earners or changes in workforce composition. Do not compare full-time adult earnings with all-employee earnings without explaining the difference.
