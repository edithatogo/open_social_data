# Accessible Guide to Labour Force, Australia

## What This Data Is About

This data describes employment, unemployment, and participation in the Australian labour market.

## Why It Matters

It helps show whether people are finding work and how labour-market conditions change across time and places.

## Key Things to Know

Counts and rates are different. A count shows people; a rate is a percentage. Also check whether the series is original, seasonally adjusted, or trend.
