# Open Social Datasets Initiative

## Product Vision
The Open Social Datasets Initiative is a centralized, open-source hub designed to make public social data from national statistics agencies (such as Stats NZ and ABS) accessible, understandable, and highly usable. 

By employing a bleeding-edge, revolutionary approach powered by **Rust**, the platform aims to provide ultra-fast, memory-safe, and highly concurrent tools for downloading, parsing, and transforming large-scale social datasets into modern formats like Parquet.

## Target Audience
- **Researchers & Data Analysts:** Academic and professional users requiring clean, well-structured, and documented datasets for complex analysis.
- **Journalists, Policymakers & Civic Developers:** Professionals searching for reliable, verifiable public statistics and open APIs to build dashboards or inform public policy.
- **General Public & Students:** Individuals looking to explore social data through accessible, plain-language guides.

## Core Pillars
1. **High-Performance Rust Tooling:** Utilizing Rust's safety and speed to build data pipes that process gigabytes of public agency data efficiently.
2. **Metadata & Data Dictionaries:** Comprehensive documentation of variables, formats, and classifications.
3. **Plain Language Guides:** Ensuring that dataset insights are accessible to non-technical users.
